Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Vision Connects is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our learning management platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including account information (name, email, password), profile details (phone, address, skills, resume), course enrollments and payment information, and communications.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, process enrollments and payments, connect students with trainers and recruiters, send notices and updates, and ensure security.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trainers for enrolled courses, recruiters when you apply for jobs, and service providers under strict confidentiality.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your account and personal data through your profile settings or by contacting us.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@visionconnects.com.
